    About Energy Generation

    Energy Generation is a pan-African organization that supports young Africans in addressing their generation's most pressing challenges (in energy, agriculture, health...) through entrepreneurship and technology.


    Energy Generation aims to support and promote technological and entrepreneurial innovation in order to identify, develop and spread "made in Africa" hardware solutions.


    First and foremost, Energy Generation is a human centered platform offering individuals opportunities to unlock and express their full creative potential..
    MISSION & VISION

    At Energy Generation we are convinced that African youth have the potential to solve the most pressing challenges of their generation (access to clean and sustainable energy, food security and access to health for all), in one generation. We want to encourage entrepreneurial projects led by young Africans and promote innovative technological solutions “made in Africa” and adapted to the continent.

    OUR BUSINESS SCHOOL

    Our Business Schools are places where theory meets practice in order to turn innovative ideas into viable businesses.

    As a student you will follow an outstanding entrepreneurial degree course and will have the opportunity to learn from the experience of field professionals from world leading compagnies in their sector.

    While at the Business Schools you will have the opportunity to develop your prototype in our equipped lab and will benefit from personalised coaching to structure your entrepreneurial project.

    SOLAR ENTREPRENEURSHIP TRAINING

    As solar represents a real opportunity to create jobs for young people while participating in the socio-economic development of African rural areas in particular, Energy Generation has created a ten-month programme aimed at training and supporting solar entrepreneurs.


    Whatever level of education you are currently in, the Solar Entrepreneurship Training offers you a tailor-made programme to guarantee you a training and support system that perfectly meets your current needs and your future plans.

    THE ENERGY SPACE

    -INCUBATOR & SEED FUND-

    The Energy Generation Incubator and Co-working space hosts promising start-ups in the field of energy, and more.

    The incubation services include personalized coaching, training and mentoring, as well as support on the startup's day-to-day activities and challenges.

    In order to support the development of an international entrepreneurial ecosystem, the incubator will welcome projects from the Business & Energy School but also several projects from diverse backgrounds and African countries.
