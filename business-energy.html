    The Business & Energy School trains Africa's leaders of tomorrow in the fields of energy, technological innovation, and entrepreneurship.
    By joining our Pan-African school, you will obtain an internationally recognized diploma while participating in the continent's energy transition through an innovative entrepreneurial project.

    Benefit from a program based on a holistic approach

    1.
    A training course developed in partnership with Ascencia Business School, a member of Collège de Paris, to acquire strong business management and project management knomwledge.
    2.
    A unique programme, based on MIT's Design Thinking method to develop your Minimum Viable Product from a technical (prototype) and commercial (business model) point of view.
    3.
    An opportunity to obtain funding designed specifically to support the initiation and large-scale marketing of your project through the Energy Generation Seed Fund.
    The Business & Energy School trains you to become an entrepreneur in the renewable energies field in Africa.
    Whatever your current level of education, the Business & Energy School offers you a training program adapted and designed to accompany you to the next level.
    Our programs are delivered in French on our campus in Lomé (Togo), and in English on our campus in Accra (Ghana).

    Business & Energy School!
    Our ambition is to provide excellent training to the brightest and most innovative minds across the continent who want to have a real positive impact on Africa's development.
    Our selection process is particularly demanding. Although academic and professional backgrounds are considered, we pay particular attention to the candidate's personality and motivation.

    1. Pre-registration

    Let's get to know you!
    Fill out the pre-registration form to receive more information about the program that suits you best.

    2. Application

    The application file consists of:
    • Your Curriculum Vitae
    • A copy of your last diploma and your last transcripts
    • Information details of a person willing to support your application
    • Short reflection essays

    3. Selection

    After studying the application files, the pre-selected candidates will be invited for a second selection phase during which they will have to:
    • Share a short presentation video
    • Pass a logic test online.

    4. Admission

    Applicants selected at the end of this selection process will be invited to complete the registration procedures. The next academic year will start in January 2022.
